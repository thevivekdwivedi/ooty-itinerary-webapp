Customized Itineraries for Bangalore → Ooty Road‑Trip (18–20 Oct 2025)

Overview – route, weather and car suitability

Travelling from Bangalore to Ooty by car takes roughly 6–7 hours and covers about 260–280 km. The most popular route is the Mysore – Masinagudi road (Bangalore → Mandya → Mysore → Nanjangud → Gundlupet → Bandipur → Mudumalai → Ooty). This route offers scenic landscapes but includes forested hills with hairpin bends. An alternative via Gudalur (Bandipur → Gudalur → Ooty) has smoother roads and fewer hairpins.

Mid‑October weather in Ooty is cool and pleasant: daytime highs around 21 °C and nights around 12 °C with frequent showers. Pack light woollens and rain gear.

Destiny – The Farm Resort

Price & pet policy. Destiny offers farm‑style cottages around US $95 per night and allows pets for an extra fee (₹1 500 per pet per night with kennel or ₹2 500 with meals). Cottages can accommodate four adults.

Why stay here? This working farm near Avalanche Lake features horses, cows, vegetable gardens, fishing and nature trails. It’s ideal if you prefer a countryside experience away from town.

Suggested itinerary (with times)

  1. 18 Oct – Travel & check‑in. 06:30 depart Bangalore; breakfast at Ramanagara or Channapatna. 11:00 visit Mysore Palace. 14:30 arrive via Gudalur and check in to Destiny. 15:00 explore the farm (animal feeding, horse rides). 19:00 enjoy a bonfire dinner.
  2. 19 Oct – Avalanche & farm activities. 08:00 breakfast. 09:00 jeep excursion to Avalanche Lake and Emerald Dam. 12:30 return for lunch. 14:00 farm tours, archery or spa treatments. 18:00 barbecue dinner under the stars.
  3. 20 Oct – Return. 08:00 breakfast and check‑out. 10:00 stop at Ooty Lake for boating. 11:30 quick visit to the Botanical Garden or Tea Factory. 13:00 depart for Bangalore.

Sterling Ooty Fern Hill

Price & pet policy. Rooms range from US $42 to $87 per night; privilege suites accommodate two adults and two children with a pet fee of about ₹2 000 per pet per day.

Why stay here? Located on a quiet hill 1.8 km from town, the resort offers a spa, steam room, gardens, evening entertainment and easy access to Ooty’s sights.

Suggested itinerary (with times)

  1. 18 Oct – Travel & check‑in. 06:30 depart Bangalore. 12:30 arrive at Sterling Fern Hill and check in. 13:00 lunch. 14:00 stroll through the resort’s gardens. 16:00 visit Ooty Lake for boating. 18:30 return for dinner and a cultural show.
  2. 19 Oct – Town sightseeing. 07:30 breakfast. 08:30 Botanical Garden. 10:00 Government Rose Garden. 12:00 Doddabetta Peak. 13:30 lunch in town. 15:00 Tea Factory. 16:30 return for games and bonfire. 19:00 dinner.
  3. 20 Oct – Return. 08:00 breakfast and check‑out. 09:00 drive back to Bangalore via Mysore or Nanjangud (optional lunch stop in Mysore).

Sherlock (Little Earth)

Price & pet policy. Rooms cost roughly US $65–130 per night. The "Paws & Play" programme welcomes pets and offers pet meals on request.

Why stay here? A Victorian‑style mansion on Tiger Hill with a large garden, reading room and fireplaces. Staff arrange heritage walks and baking sessions.

Suggested itinerary (with times)

  1. 18 Oct – Check‑in & heritage walk. 15:00 arrive and check in to Sherlock. 15:30 join a heritage walk through nearby tea estates and colonial bungalows. 17:00 enjoy high tea. 18:30 dinner by the fireplace.
  2. 19 Oct – Town exploration. 07:30 breakfast. 08:30 Ooty Lake. 09:30 St Stephen’s Church. 10:30 Botanical Garden. 12:30 lunch in town. 14:00 Tea Factory. 16:00 Doddabetta Peak. 18:00 relax in the garden or reading room.
  3. 20 Oct – Return. 08:00 breakfast, check out and drive back to Bangalore.

Savoy – IHCL SeleQtions

Price & pet policy. Rooms cost about US $198–343 per night. Pets are allowed for a fee (≈ ₹3 500 per night plus deposit). The hotel includes free breakfast, an outdoor pool and gardens.
