Bangalore → Kabini Road‑Trip Itinerary (21–22 Oct 2025)

Overview – route, weather and car suitability

The drive from Bangalore to Kabini via the Bengaluru–Mysuru Expressway covers roughly 220 km and takes around 5–6 hours【559783900588386†L90-L100】. The expressway is smooth and toll‑gated, but the final 10 km near Kabini can be rough and unpaved【559783900588386†L141-L149】, so your Skoda Superb should proceed carefully. Late October is post‑monsoon: expect warm days around 28–30 °C with overcast skies and short drizzles【283180612983399†L173-L177】; the heavy monsoon rains subside after September【792262624541937†L65-L72】. This itinerary assumes you depart on the morning of 21 Oct 2025 and return by evening on 22 Oct 2025; adjust times to suit your pace.

Red Earth Kabini

Price & pet policy. This eco‑friendly lodge sits on the backwaters of the Kabini River. Expect to pay around US $200–250 per night (≈₹16 000–20 000) per cottage for two adults. Amenities include an outdoor pool, spa, garden and restaurant offering local cuisine as well as ecotours and fishing【173492359417349†L264-L267】. Major travel sites note that the property features a pool, spa, bar, breakfast options and a 24/7 front desk【173492359417349†L260-L289】. Pets are welcome for a fee of about ₹1 000 per pet per night, and buffet breakfast costs around ₹400 per adult【173492359417349†L899-L904】【173492359417349†L960-L962】. Availability: Online searches show several cottages still available for the weekend of 21–22 Oct 2025, but the resort often fills up quickly – secure your booking as soon as possible.

Why stay here? Red Earth Kabini offers a tranquil retreat amid nature. Travellers praise its natural setting and opportunities to observe wildlife right from the property【173492359417349†L264-L267】. Older guests appreciate the on‑site spa and relaxed ambience, while younger visitors can enjoy fishing, ecotours, kayaking and other activities【173492359417349†L264-L267】. The resort’s pet‑friendly policy and eco‑friendly ethos make it ideal for a group that includes multiple generations and a dog.

All‑inclusive options? Red Earth packages usually include accommodation and meals; some packages add boat or jeep safaris. For a true all‑inclusive safari package with meals and both boat and jeep safaris, consider Kabini River Lodge (Jungle Lodges & Resorts), which offers a single price covering accommodation, buffet meals, an evening boat safari and a morning jeep safari【637043613338182†L100-L104】【950518261969170†L166-L170】.
