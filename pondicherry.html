Bangalore → Pondicherry Road‑Trip Itinerary (18–20 Oct 2025)

Overview – route, weather and car suitability

The drive from Bangalore to Pondicherry via NH 77 is about 300 km and takes roughly seven hours. Travel guides note that this highway is excellent with smooth tarmac and minimal traffic【791641356226642†L99-L107】【791641356226642†L145-L147】. Mid‑October brings warm, humid weather in Pondicherry: daytime highs around 32 °C and lows around 25–26 °C with isolated thunderstorms. Forecasts estimate about 9.6 mm of rain on 18 Oct, 4.8 mm on 19 Oct and 8.7 mm on 20 Oct【670591262873256†L144-L157】. Pack light cottons, sun protection and umbrellas.

This plan assumes departure on 18 Oct 2025 and return on 20 Oct 2025. Feel free to adjust times to suit your pace.

Radisson Resort Pondicherry Bay

Price & pet policy. Rates typically start around US $140–$180 per night. The resort welcomes pets (dogs and cats) and offers dedicated pet‑friendly chalets【895682486273574†L52-L82】【895682486273574†L96-L105】.

Why stay here? Nestled on the backwaters near Paradise Beach, the Radisson blends Franco‑Tamil culture with modern comforts. Chalets and villas feature verandas overlooking the lagoon, while amenities include a spa, swimming pool, kids’ club, water sports, and two restaurants serving Franco‑Tamil fusion cuisine【895682486273574†L52-L82】【895682486273574†L96-L105】. Its relaxed setting is perfect for older travellers seeking tranquility as well as younger couples looking for beach fun.

Suggested itinerary (with times)

  1. 18 Oct – Drive & beach relaxation. 05:30 depart Bangalore; stop for breakfast near Krishnagiri. 12:30 arrive at Radisson Resort Pondicherry Bay and check in; enjoy lunch. 15:00 stroll along Paradise Beach or unwind at the spa and pool. 19:00 dinner at the resort.
  2. 19 Oct – Heritage & spirituality. 08:00 breakfast. 09:00 join a heritage walk through White Town (the French Quarter). 11:00 visit Sri Aurobindo Ashram. 12:30 lunch at a Franco‑Tamil café. 13:00 drive to Auroville to view the Matrimandir. 15:00 explore the Pondicherry Museum or browse Mission Street shops. 16:30 enjoy sunset at Promenade Beach. 19:00 return for a seafood dinner.
  3. 20 Oct – Water sports & return. 07:30 breakfast. 08:30 head to Chunnambar Boat House for a boat ride, kayaking or paddle‑boarding (weather permitting). 10:00 check out. 11:00 begin the drive back to Bangalore with a lunch stop near Krishnagiri. Reach home by 18:00.

Note: Weather and traffic conditions can change unexpectedly. Check live updates before starting and carry sun hats, water and rain gear for a comfortable journey.
