Bangalore → Hampi Road‑Trip Itinerary (18–20 Oct 2025)

Overview – route, weather and car suitability

Driving from Bangalore to Hampi covers roughly 350 km via NH 48 and NH 50 and takes about six hours. Recent travel guides note that this is the fastest route with good road conditions and standard tolls【632567427609919†L61-L67】【632567427609919†L103-L107】. Mid‑October weather in Hampi is warm with occasional showers: forecasts predict around 0.41 inch of rain on 18 Oct, 0.24 inch on 19 Oct and 0.06 inch on 20 Oct【369438450676606†L151-L160】, so pack light cottons and a rain jacket.

This itinerary assumes departure on the morning of 18 Oct 2025 and return by evening on 20 Oct 2025. Adjust timings based on your pace and interests.

Hyatt Place Hampi

Price & pet policy. Rooms start around US $120 per night. The hotel is pet‑friendly, allowing dogs and cats for a small fee【60276096451320†L218-L223】. Rooms are spacious with plunge pools and modern amenities. Note on inclusions: Hyatt Place is not an all‑inclusive property – meals and beverages are à la carte. Travellers seeking a fully inclusive stay can opt for the nearby Hampi Heritage & Wilderness Resort (Jungle Lodges), which advertises an all‑inclusive package with meals and safari【950518261969170†L166-L170】.

Why stay here? Set amid tranquil gardens near the ancient ruins, Hyatt Place Hampi offers 24/7 dining, an outdoor pool, fitness centre and attentive service【60276096451320†L218-L223】. The relaxed pace and accessible facilities make it ideal for both older travellers and younger couples. Availability: When we checked major booking platforms for 18–20 Oct 2025 (two rooms for four adults), the hotel still showed rooms available, but inventory was limited. We recommend securing your booking early.

Suggested itinerary (with times)

  1. 18 Oct – Drive & check‑in. 06:00 depart Bangalore and enjoy breakfast en‑route near Tumkur. 11:00 explore the historic Chitradurga Fort. 14:00 arrive at Hyatt Place Hampi in Vidyanagar and check in; have lunch. 15:30 relax by the pool or book a spa treatment. 19:00 savour dinner at the hotel.
  2. 19 Oct – Explore Hampi’s ruins. 07:30 breakfast. 08:30 visit Virupaksha Temple and wander the Hampi Bazaar. 10:00 explore the Elephant Stables and Lotus Mahal. 12:30 lunch at a local café. 14:00 tour the Vithala Temple and admire the Stone Chariot. 16:30 enjoy sunset views – choose either Matanga Hill for a moderate trek or a riverside spot for a gentler option. 18:30 return to the hotel for dinner.
  3. 20 Oct – Museums & return. 07:30 breakfast. 08:30 discover artefacts at the Archaeological Museum in Kamalapur. 10:00 stroll around the Tungabhadra Dam and gardens. 12:00 depart for Bangalore with a lunch stop on the way. Arrive home around 18:00.

Note: Weather and driving conditions can change; check real‑time forecasts and traffic before departure. Always carry sufficient water and snacks for the long drive.
